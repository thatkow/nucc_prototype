What membership looks like

Our trips run roughly every fortnight during the university semester and welcome all levels of experience. We offer help and advice with the technical side of caving and provide all the required gear. The most important thing a member ever needs to bring along on a trip is themselves!

From day trips through weekend adventures to longer expeditions, we head underground as often as possible. Weekend and longer trips often include camping in the amazing Australian bush — usually with a roaring campfire — or staying in relaxed caver accommodation like huts.

We car pool to and from caving sites departing from ANU Sport, so you do not need a car to join. Fuel costs are shared between trip attendees.

Trip Costs

Costs are kept as low as possible — we are mostly poor, broke uni students after all! Expect up to four components:

  • Shared equipment maintenance ($5 when using club gear)
  • Personal gear hire, including an SRT kit if needed ($5)
  • Shared fuel costs
  • Camping/accommodation fees (if applicable)
  • Park access fees (if applicable)

Usually this adds up to $10–$20 for a day trip, and $25–$50 for an overnight or longer trip, depending on location. Compared with commercial caving tours ($150–$200 for a half-day), NUCC trips are incredible value.

Membership Fees

Yearly fees include ANU Sport insurance and gear maintenance:

  • $55 per year for ANU students
  • $75 + $120 club affiliation fee for non-students

Your membership unlocks every trip, training session, and expedition we run for a full rolling year.

Membership benefits

  • ✓ Regular caving and canyoning trips — most beginner friendly
  • ✓ Weekly Single Rope Technique (SRT) training to master vertical caves
  • ✓ Access to permit-only caves the public will never see
  • ✓ Low cost day trips ($10–$20) and weekend trips ($25–$50)
  • ✓ Overseas and interstate expeditions — recent destinations include New Zealand and Tasmania
  • ✓ No experience or equipment required — we provide helmets, headtorches, and more

How to join

  1. Navigate to NUCC on UniOne, ANU Sport’s club membership website. Create an account if you do not already have one.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and select the appropriate membership option:
    • ANU students: 2023 Caving Student and Life Members Membership
    • Non-students and non-ANU students: 2023 Caving Associate Membership
  3. Fill out all online forms and pay through UniOne.
  4. You’re ready to go! Membership lasts for one rolling year.
